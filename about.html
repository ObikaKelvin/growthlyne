We, at Growth Lyne are a team based in Bangalore of experienced professionals having multiple years of knowledge at our behest, who have a thorough comprehension of the accuracy and efficiency that is imperative for offering time driven solutions to preempt, parallel or precede the current hyper competitive environment that industries are existing in. We also understand that industries establish, function and fight to grow in these environments. At the basic fabric of every vision is the desire to win and not mere existence.

We, as an organization, provide to these very needs. Growth Lyne specializes in offering all-inclusive end – to - end solutions for all Marketing Research and Analytics Projects. We offer specialized packages in the entire spectrum of the field and are known for our command on each and every aspect of Market Research. We have dedicated teams that work with our clients as an integral, non - differentiated department of their organization to understand the requirement of our clients. These teams work with the client exclusively to fulfill all sub tasks of the project, starting from Data Collection, Data Processing, Data Analysis to the final sub project of Data Reporting.

Through our presence of X number of years in the Market Research and Analytics industry, we have worked with various organizations that are leading in their respective fields. Our portfolio of work ranges from Healthcare, Consumer, Media, X, Y, Z, A, B, C to Retail and many other industries. In all our projects we, at Growth Lyne, have worked towards creating innovative and unparalleled solutions for our clients that are deployable in the real time environments of the current world’s fast changing economy.

Our understanding of core industry practices, market dynamics, business functions, along with the experience of our team and our expertise in managing the complex tools utilized for solutioning for modern day businesses makes us a one stop customized solution provider for all strategic, functional and / or consumer analytics needs that an organization can face in the course of its life. Our experiences and multiple collaborations with organizations of our industry across the globe have added to our intellectual resources and made us a leading company.

Growth Lyne teams are known for their core values of Integrity, Equality, Excellence, Constant Improvisation and Customer Focus. And it is these core values, along with our intelligence pools, that have helped in steering the organization to benchmarks that are synonymous with excellence.
